Mentorship Program 2015: Summer Project

I am leading a mentorship intiative as part of Rachel Naomi Design for select students. Below is a curriculum that is 2 months long for my Mentee.

Goal

Design a product based on one avatar from a game

Choose product that the avatar would use for fun, everyday living, or work. Build a brand off of the product

Design and create

  • User persona
    • Usability.gov/how-to-and-tools/methods/personas.html
    • Background
    • Goals
    • Values
    • Answer as many questions from
  • Inspiration Board (Pinterest board is OK @raybeezdesign)
    • Elements that Inspire the design and concepts
  • Mood board
    • Elements that help build and define the brand
    • Colors based on persona
  • Logo for product
  • Brand guide (web or print)
    • Consider items for web and print that should be thought out before designing
    • Colors in Pantone Solid Coated, CMYK, RGB, HEX
  • Product: Package or app
    • Go simple as possible in concept
  • Responsive website (design in Illustrator, Photoshop, or Sketch mobile and desktop. Start with mobile)
    • Home page
    • About page
    • Product page

Deadlines and Deliverables

Skype, Google Hangout, or physically meet 6pm Eastern std time.

Bring computer or tablet.

June 29th:

  • Begin the Work

July 14th:

  • Logo and product brainstorm with mind mapping, sketches, and any other notes for the elements
  • Inspiration board: 5 or more research sites and/or books for each required deliverable that taught or inspired (pinterest)
  • Mood board
  • 3 High fidelity logo mockups of each. Both B/W and color (PDF display)
  • Beginning of brand guide

July 27th:

  • Final logo: Pantone Solid Coated, CMYK, RGB, HEX (PDF Display and print logo)
  • Final Brand guide (PDF)
  • Product brainstorm of mind mapping (look it up if don’t know) and sketches
  • 3 High fidelity Mock ups of product (PDF or Invisionapp)
  • Sketches of website wire frame. Structure of web elements on the page. The Bones.

August 5th:

  • 3 high fidelity versions of mock ups of website’s 3 pages
    • Utilize Invisionapp. Link your .pngs and .jpgs
    • Share and comments for explaining ideas

August 24th:

  • Final Presentation with Resume updated
    • Present with logos and brand guide while using the persona to explain your process of final design for the company
    • Present Product and Website

Final Expectations:

  • Student is excited about the project
  • Well thought out brand and product
  • Experimental with imagery and typography
  • Show growth and learning through the project
  • Have a piece Student is proud to show in his portfolio
  • Present confidently (no ums, likes, or just)
  • Update Resume and Linkedin (If not on Linkedin, Join)
  • This list might grow as the project continues…
  • Learn new skills
  • Feel more confident in design
  • Design is current and on trend

Glossary/References

  • High Fidelity
    • Close to final design
  • Mindmapping
    • bubble ideas with divergent thinking
    • https://bubbl.us/ or sketchbook
  • Moodboard: more focused honed in inspiration board
    • http://www.gomoodboard.com/ or pinterest
  • Invisionapp
    • Free for one project
    • If your product is digital utilize one project for product and website
    • Divide projects by images
  • User Persona
    • The purpose of personas is to create reliable and realistic representations of your key audience segments for reference. These representations should be based on qualitative and some quantitative user research and web analytics. Remember, your personas are only as good as the research behind them.
    • http://www.usability.gov/how-to-and-tools/methods/personas.html
