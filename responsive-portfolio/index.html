Rachel Naomi Beser

I am a Web Designer for Tenable Network Security with four years experiences designing for users. I design and develop front-end web solutions for tenable.com, our emails, and interior monitor signage. I graduated from Towson University with a B.S. in Art and Design with a Concentration in Graphic Design in 2010. At Towson I took extra web courses, continued taking courses at CCBC after graduation, and learned front-end web development from online tutorials and various blogs. I've worked for the U.S. Army, Tech Start Ups, Towson University, and an ad agency. My career has ranged from print, production, social media, web design, and to front-end web development. I am also an active member in the design community through AIGA (Professional Association for Design) and meetups. I served on the AIGA Baltimore board from 2011-2013.

If you want to contact me, you can reach me at rachelnaomidesign@gmail.com
