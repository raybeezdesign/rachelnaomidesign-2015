Rachel Naomi Beser Brookes

I am a User Experience Designer for the Federal Reserve Bank of Kansas City with over five years experience designing for users. I have designed and developed front-end web solutions for tenable.com, and other entities in the past. I graduated from Towson University with a B.S. in Art and Design with a Concentration in Graphic Design in 2010. At Towson I took extra web courses, continued taking courses at CCBC after graduation, and learned front-end web development from online tutorials and various blogs. I've worked for Tenable Network Security, the U.S. Army, Tech Start Ups, Towson University, and ad and marketing agencies. My career has ranged from print, production, social media, web design, and to front-end web development. I am also an active member in the design community through AIGA (Professional Association for Design) and meetups. I served on the AIGA Baltimore board from 2011-2013.

If you want to contact me, you can reach me at rachelnaomidesign[at]gmail[dot]com
